what is Fatty liver?

Fatty liver happens when too much fat builds up in your liver cells, which can lead to inflammation and potential damage to your liver. This condition is often linked to lifestyle factors like being overweight, eating an unhealthy diet, drinking too much alcohol, or having certain medical issues.
