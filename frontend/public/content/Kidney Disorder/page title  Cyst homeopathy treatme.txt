what is Cyst?

A cyst is like a small pouch that can develop in different parts of your body. Think of it as a tiny, closed bubble filled with fluid, air, or other substances. It's similar to a balloon, but it stays inside you rather than floating away! These cysts can range from being very tiny to quite large, and they can pop up almost anywhere, like on your skin, in your ovaries, kidneys, or even in your brain.

While some cysts don't cause any trouble and might vanish on their own, others can cause discomfort, pain, or other issues depending on their size and where they are located. It's crucial to have them checked by a doctor if they start causing symptoms or if you notice any changes in your body.
