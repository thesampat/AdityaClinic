what is Migraine?

A migraine is more than just a headache; it's a neurological condition that brings intense headaches, often accompanied by other symptoms like nausea, sensitivity to light and sound, and visual disturbances. These headaches can be debilitating, affecting daily activities and overall quality of life. People experiencing migraines may feel throbbing or pulsating pain on one side of the head, and attacks can last from a few hours to several days. Migraines can vary widely among individuals, with triggers ranging from stress and hormonal changes to certain foods and environmental factors. Managing migraines often requires a personalized approach to address triggers and symptoms effectively.
