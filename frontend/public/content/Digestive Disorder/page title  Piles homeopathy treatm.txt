what is Piles?

Piles, commonly known as hemorrhoids, are swollen and inflamed veins located in the rectum and anus. They can cause discomfort, pain, itching, and sometimes bleeding during bowel movements. Piles can be internal, occurring inside the rectum, or external, forming under the skin around the anus. Factors like straining during bowel movements, chronic constipation or diarrhea, pregnancy, and a sedentary lifestyle can contribute to the development of piles. Homeopathy offers gentle and personalized treatment options to relieve symptoms, reduce inflammation, and address the underlying causes of piles, providing long-term relief without invasive procedures or harsh medications.
