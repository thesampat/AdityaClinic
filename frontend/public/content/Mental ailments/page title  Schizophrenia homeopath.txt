what is Schizophrenia?

Schizophrenia is a mental health condition that affects how a person thinks, feels, and acts. It can cause big changes in how someone sees reality, their emotions, and their thoughts. For example, someone with schizophrenia might hear voices or believe things that aren't true.

This condition usually shows up when people are young adults, and it can make daily life quite challenging. It's important to understand that schizophrenia is not the same as having multiple personalities. Instead, it brings a mix of symptoms that can be different for each person.

Living with schizophrenia can make it tough to interact with others, handle work or school, and take care of oneself. Treatment usually involves a mix of medicines, therapy, and making lifestyle changes. Getting diagnosed early and getting the right treatment can make a big difference in improving someone's life with schizophrenia.
